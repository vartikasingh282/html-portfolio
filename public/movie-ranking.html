The Best Movie According to Vartika Singh.

My top 3 movies Of all-times

  • Dangal

    The movie plot is based on true story. Mahavir Singh Phogat, a former wrestler, decides to fulfil his dream of winning a gold medal for his country by training his daughters for the Commonwealth Games despite the existing social stigmas.
    In my opinion - This movie will inspire whoever watch it.

  • 3 Idiots

    The movie teaches us that a certificate is nothing more than a piece of paper. Real education is to acquire knowledge. After graduation, Rancho gives away his certificate to someone else, and with the help of his acquired knowledge, he became a great scientist.
    In my opinion - In this movie you will learn about life on another level.

  • Taare Zameen Par

    Ishaan is criticised by his parents for his poor academic performance and is sent away to a boarding school. Ram, an art teacher, however, realises he has dyslexia and helps him uncover his potential.
    In my opinion - This movie talk about mental health in a very good way.
